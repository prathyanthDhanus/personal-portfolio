Introduction

Hi, I'm Prathyanth Dhanus, a Full Stack Developer based in Kannur, Kerala. I specialize in creating dynamic and beautiful web applications that deliver excellent user experiences.

Background and Experience

I hold a diploma in Electronics from Government ITI Kannur, which I completed in 2020. After earning my diploma, I worked as a Service Engineer with Sony Service for over two years. During this time, I gained valuable technical skills and hands-on experience in troubleshooting and repair.

Recognizing the vast opportunities in the IT field, I decided to pivot my career and completed a 6-month internship at BridgeOn Solutions in Malappuram. This experience provided me with a solid foundation in web development and introduced me to the exciting world of coding.

Currently, I am working as a Junior MERN Stack Developer at Kodlar Innovations LLP in Malappuram on a contract basis. I started my journey with Kodlar in January 2024, and it has been an incredible learning experience. Working alongside experienced developers, I have gained exposure to new technologies and their practical applications, which have significantly enhanced my skills and knowledge.

Skills and Expertise

I am proficient in JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, and Express.js. My adaptability and time management skills make me a valuable team player. My self-learning ability helps me quickly acquire new technologies. I specialize in both frontend and backend development, with a focus on responsive design and performance optimization.

Professional Philosophy

I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and continuously learning new technologies to stay ahead in the fast-paced tech world. My approach involves a deep understanding of user needs, collaborative problem-solving, and a commitment to delivering high-quality solutions.

Personal Interests

Outside of work, I enjoy swimming, photography, and driving. I also do physical workouts, which make my body and mind more flexible and give me a relaxed mind. This helps me reduce stress and come up with new ideas. I am passionate about open-source software and help my colleagues solve their errors. I also give advice to my juniors.
